About
Me.

Hi, I'm Matt — a Melbourne-based 3D artist and UX/UI designer with a passion for crafting believable worlds and intuitive experiences.

My 3D work spans environment art, prop modelling, and real-time rendering — always with game performance in mind. On the UX side, I'm drawn to research-led design: getting out of the building, talking to real people, and letting those insights shape the product.

In my free time, I enjoy geeking out about video and board games. If I'm not out and about eating, you will definitely catch me at home drawing, gaming or baking!

Feel free to reach out to me and have a chat about working together or geeking out!
